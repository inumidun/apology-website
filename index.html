A Letter From My Heart

Dear Buslat,

I know I hurt you, and there are no words that can fully undo the pain I caused. But I want you to know that I carry it with me — not as a burden, but as a reminder of how much you mean to me.

You have given me everything — your time, your patience, your unconditional love. And in return, I gave you a moment of carelessness that you didn't deserve. I am deeply, truly sorry.

You are the strongest, most loving person I know. You taught me kindness, resilience, and what it means to love without limits. I only hope I can spend the rest of my life living up to the person you raised me to be.

I don't ask you to forget. I only ask that, when your heart is ready, you let me back in — so I can show you, every single day, how grateful I am to call you my mother.

With all my love, always 💗
Oyindamola
